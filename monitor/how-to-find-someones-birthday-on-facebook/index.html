Part One: Four Common and Simple Methods to Find Someone's Birthday on Facebook

Method 1: Checking Someone's Birthday with Facebook Events

Finding someone's birthday is made easier thanks to Facebook. You can simply visit their profile and check it. Alternatively, you can find this information in the events section of your profile. Simply log in to your account using the Facebook mobile app or desktop site.

For Mobile App Users

Step 1: Tap to open the Facebook app on your device and Log in using your credentials.

Step 2: Tap on the search button on the screen top and Key in Birthdays. Then, tap on that option.

Step 3: Your friend's birthdays will pop up as recent and upcoming events.

For Desktop Website Users

Step 1: Visit the Facebook site and log in with your credentials.

Step 2: On the left sidebar, look for "Events" and click it.

Step 3: Thereafter, choose "Birthdays" from the options.

Step 4: You can now identify someone's birthday. Also, you'll see the recent and upcoming birthdays of your friends.

Method 2: Knowing Someone's Birthday with Facebook Personal Profile

Finding someone's birthday isn't that complicated if you know where to look. Simply go to their Facebook Personal Profile and you'll be able to see it.

Step 2: Visit their profile and Tap About

Besides the birthday, the About section also reveals other important details. Of course, not all of them will be accurate. Your friend might have lied in some parts. The information includes education, history, work, and where they live.

Method 3: Seeing Someone's Birthday with Facebook Friends Page

If you can't find your friend's birthday in the notification section, then it's not on that day. So, what should you do next? Well, that means your friends might be coming up at a later date. And here's how to see it, especially if it's listed on the platform.

Steps:How to See Someone's Birthday on Facebook

Step 2: Thereafter, look for "Friends" in the list of options and click it and Now look for "Birthdays" and tap it

Step 3: From the results available, you can look through the Friends list to discover upcoming birthdays.
