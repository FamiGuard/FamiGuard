Method 1: Read Snap Messages Without Sender Knowing By Using Airplane Mode

One way to read Snapchat messages without the sender is by using Airplane Mode. When you are in Airplane Mode, your phone will not connect to any network, preventing the sender from knowing that you have read their message.

This is how you can do it:

  1. Turn on Airplane Mode
  2. Once Airplane Mode is turned on, open the Snapchat conversation and read the message.

It's the most effective way to read a message without the sender knowing. However, sometimes if you haven't already loaded the conversation, you may not be able to see it cause when you're on Airplane mode, the internet is off.

Keep in mind that this method will not work if the sender is viewing your "Story" or if they have already seen the message.

Method 2: Half Swiping

Another way to read Snapchat messages without the sender knowing is by using Half Swiping. In a nutshell, Snapchat tells senders if their messages have been opened. If you slide it near the 3/4th of the screen, you may read someone's Snapchat message without them knowing.

This is how you can do it:

  1. Swipe right on the Snapchat app to access your conversations. On your friends' conversation page, look for a New Snap label (if it's a snap) or a New Chat label (if it's a chat message) beneath your pals' name.
  2. Swipe right on the conversation you want to look at slowly and carefully. Don't swipe all the way right. Remember that if you're reading the message, don't release your finger.
  3. When you can read some of the messages, keep swiping slowly while holding below until a little border appears on the right side of the screen. You'll be able to understand this part of the conversation only.

This is a difficult method, and the success rate is low. We don't recommend it.
