Part 1: How to See Someone's Most Recent Posts on Instagram?

If you want to take a look at someone's most recent posts on Instagram, follow these steps:

Step 1: First, open the Instagram app and log in.

Step 2: Next, go to the profile page of the person whose posts you want to see.

Step 3: Once you're on their profile page, you'll see their most recent posts at the top of the screen. You can scroll through their posts by swiping up or down.

And that's all you need to do!

Part 2: How to See When Someone is Online on Instagram?

Unfortunately, there is no way to see when someone was last active on Instagram if they have their activity status turned off.

If the person you're trying to track has their activity status turned on. However, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: First, open the Instagram app and log in.

Step 2: Next, go to the profile page of the person you want to track.

Step 3: On their profile page, you should see their activity status next to their name and bio (it will say 'active now' or 'active ___ ago').

On some versions of Instagram, you may see a green dot next to someone's name online.

That's all you need to do!

Part 3: How to See Who Someone Recently Followed on Instagram?

To see who someone recently followed on Instagram:

Step 1: Open the app and log in.

Step 2: Next, go to the profile page of the person you want to track. Scroll down and tap Following > Recent.

Step 3: Tap a username to view that person's profile. You can also tap Following > Search to find a specific person that someone follows.

And that's all you need to do! By following these steps, you'll see who someone recently followed on Instagram.
