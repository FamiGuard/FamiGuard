Solution 1: Use Google Maps Location Sharing to Track an Android Phone from iPhone

Google Maps allows you to share your Location with others and offers the added benefit of tracking other people's locations. It's pretty simple. Enable Google Maps' Location Sharing feature and tell the app who can see your whereabouts.

Step 1: Open up the Settings menu on your phone and scroll down until you find Google. Tap it and then tap Location to open up Google Maps' settings.

Step 2: Then tap OK when prompted to allow Google to collect data about your Location.

Step 3: Next, go back to the main screen to set up a circle that represents all of those who will be able to track your Location.

Solution 2: Use Google Find My Device to Track an Android Phone from iPhone

Google's Find My Device app will help you find your lost phone. You can use this app to ring or lock your lost device remotely, making it easier for someone else who has seen it (hopefully) to contact you. There are three steps given below:

Step 1: Install the Google Find My Device app on your iPhone and Android.

Step 2: Log into the app using the same Gmail account linked to your Android phone. If you are not signed in, tap Unlink Account and sign in.

Step 3: Next, You'll see a map of the last time your device was seen on the network.

Solution 3: Use Google Maps Timeline to Track an Android Phone from iPhone

The Google Maps Timeline feature offers a simple way to track a person's movements, so you can use it to find out where they are. If you're tracking your phone with the Find My Device service, make sure your Location is set to High Accuracy in Settings. 

Step 1: Open the Google Maps app on your iPhone and sign into your account if needed.

Step 2: Tap on Explore at the bottom of the screen and then tap on Timeline

Step 3: This tab will take a few seconds to load, but once it does, tap on Location. Here you'll see all the places you've visited using this account.

Solution 4: Use WhatsApp to Track Your Android Phone

WhatsApp is a free messaging app for iPhones and Androids so you can use it on both. You can download WhatsApp for iPhone here and Android here. One of the best features of WhatsApp is its 'tracker' function, which will allow you to see your phone's Location. Here's how it works

Step 1: Open the WhatsApp and Tap on Attach File Symbol.

Step 2: After Taping this. You will see the Share Location Option.

Step 3: You are now all ready to start tracking your phone at this point.

Solution 5: Use IMEI Number to Track Your Android Phone

The IMEI number is a unique identifier for every mobile device, so it can be used to track any device that's connected to a cellular network. This number should be noted for future reference and shared with authorities in case I don't remember you finding your phone.

As for seeing and checking the IMEI number on your iPhone, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Simply go to Settings

Step 2: Tap on About Phone

Step 3: Scroll down to IMEI information

Besides, there is another way to see and check your IMEI number:

Step 1: Click on the phone icon of your Android Phone

Step 2: Tap the numeric keyboard

Step 3: *#06#(asterisk, hash, zero, six, hash).

After getting the IMEI Number, you can follow these steps to track your Android Phone:

Step 1: Download and install IMEI Tracker from your Google Store on your smartphone.

Step 2: Enter the IMEI number and click the Find my Device button.

Step 3: You will see all Locations where this specific device was last seen.

Solution 6: Make Calls or Send Messages to Find Your Android Phone

Although making calls or sending messages to the lost phone sounds like the dumbest way to track your lost Android Phone, it is actually useful and effective. If someone else picks up your lost Android Phone, then they will call you or message you. If they refuse to answer your phone call, then you certainly know that your Android Phone is picked up or stolen by someone else. Therefore, you should try other methods to get back your lost Android Phone.
