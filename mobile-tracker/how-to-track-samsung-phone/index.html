Method1: How to Track the Lost Samsung  Phone Using the "Find my Mobile" Service

If you've lost your Samsung phone and want to get it back, the "Find my Mobile" feature can help. The Find My Device Samsung is an in-built service that allows you to remotely track the location of a lost device.

The only requirement for using this method is that you must have previously set up a Samsung account on your device. If you are unsure whether this service is enabled, follow the steps below to activate it and then use it to locate your lost Samsung phone.

Step 1: On your Samsung phone, go to the "Settings" app and then tap "Biometrics and security." In the section you'll see "Find My Mobile," turn it on.

Step 2: Visit the "findmymobile.samsung.com" website from any of your web browsers and log in with your Samsung account details.

Step 3: Select the preferred device from the left menu. If your lost device is on and connected to a network, you will see an active indicator on a map showing its location.

Method2: Track Samsung Phone with "Find My Device" Features

Similar to Samsung’s “Find My Mobile”, Google also offers a free “Find My Device” service for all Android devices to help you locate, lock or erase a lost device.

As long as you've added a Google Account to your device and enabled the location access for Find My Device on your Samsung phone, you're all set.

To use this method, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Visit the "android.com/find" website from any web browser on your computer or Android.

Step 2: Log in with the Google account details that are associated with your lost Samsung device.

Step 3: Choose your preferred device from the list. You will then be provided with its current location on a map.

Method3: Using "Family Link App" to Locate  Samsung Phone

The Family Link app from Google is a great tool for parents to locate their child's phone if it is ever lost or stolen. It can also be used to track your child's location in real time by indicating their position on Google Maps.

Follow these steps to set up the Family Link app on your child's device and then use it to locate their Samsung phone:

Step 1: Download the Family Link app on your device and follow the on-screen instructions to finish the process of linking your account to your child's.

Step 2: Open the Family Link app and choose the child whose device you want to locate. Select "Set Up" from the "Location" menu, then tap "Turn on" from the popup that displays.

Step 3: Enable "See your child's location" under Manage Settings>Location. Then, under "Device location settings", turn on "Device location," and select "High accuracy." That's it!

Method5: Track your Samsung phone with the IMEI number

Every phone has a unique IMEI (International Mobile Equipment Identity) number which can be used to track the phone if it is ever lost or stolen.

There are many ways to track a Samsung phone by its IMEI number. One of the easiest is to download a free IMEI tracker app that will help you easily track your device.

Here's how to track a Samsung phone for free with the IMEI number:

Step 1: Go to the Google Play Store and search for the "IMEI tracker-Find my device" app. Install that app on any of your smartphones.

Step 2: Open the app and grant location access permission. Then enter the phone number of a trusted member.

Step 3: When your phone is lost/stolen and a new SIM card is inserted, a SMS notification with the IMEI number and exact phone location will be sent automatically to that number.

Method6: Track my Samsung phone with "Android Lost"

Android Lost is a handy app that can help you find your lost phone or erase sensitive data from it so that it doesn't fall into the wrong hands.

If your lost Samsung phone was running on an Android version lower than 3.0, then you can easily install the app on your device remotely and activate it by sending an SMS.

The steps for using Android Lost to track your Samsung phone are listed below:

Step 1: Use a browser to visit the Google Play store and install Android Lost app remotely. Ensure that your Google Account is connected to your device.

Step 2: Now arrange another device and use it to send a text “androidlost register” on to your device. This will register your device and remotely activate the app.

Step 3: Finally, go to "androidlost.com" and log in with your Google credentials. From there, you can access options like device location and data erase.
