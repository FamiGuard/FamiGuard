FAQs & Tips

1. Is iMyFone FamiGenius for WhatsApp in disguise and cannot be easily found out?

Yes, iMyFone FamiGenius for WhatsApp won't show its original app icon and it will be renamed in Settings by default. If you're a user of Android 10+, the app icon will disguise itself as internet settings.

2. If I read others' WhatsApp messages before they do, will they find out?

No, they have no idea. Because our product work in complete incognito mode and it won't trigger the WhatsApp Read Receipt mechanism.

3. What if I cancel subscription or diconnect the previous target device from my account?

All the information the server collected before will be deleted automatically. iMyFone FamiGenius for WhatsApp will stop tracking any devices. Also you can remove the app manually if you worry about privacy.
